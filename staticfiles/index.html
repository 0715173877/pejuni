Our Services

Unlocking the potential of mining projects with our expert geotechnical solutions tailored to your needs.

Geotechnical Data Collection

Pejuni provides expert core logging services essential for geotechnical design. Our team specializes in extracting and analyzing soil and rock samples to deliver precise data on subsurface conditions. This information is crucial for designing stable and reliable structures. With our advanced resources and experienced professionals, Pejuni ensures accurate and comprehensive geotechnical assessments to enhance your project’s safety and efficiency.

Learn more

Slope Stability & Pit Design

  • Pit design from data collection and data analysis to detailed design.
  • Slope stability monitoring of pit walls, waste dumps, and tailings dams using automated systems like QuikSlope.
  • Development of Slope Management Plans and Hazard Management plans.
  • Risk management to ensure safe mining operations under geotechnical-related hazards.
  • Dewatering and depressurization monitoring for the degradation of slopes.
Learn more

Soil Lab & Rock Testing

For your added convenience, we provide advanced geotechnical tests through our associated laboratory in Tanzania or at your preferred Lab. We organise data collection and ensure timely testing of the results as per the project schedule. We handle the samples and reporting exactly the same as your samples with our laboratory.

Geotechnical Training and Mentorship

The training support provided by Pejuni is comprehensive, beginning with a structured program adopted to your developmental needs. Geotechnical engineers will acquire the required technical knowledge and skills necessary to perform their duties on any challenge. The programme involves lectures, field work, case studies and relating theory to practice in real life underground.
